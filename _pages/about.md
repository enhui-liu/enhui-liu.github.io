I am Enhui Liu, a fourth-year medical student specialising in Paediatrics at **Guangzhou Medical University**. My research interests lie in **AI for healthcare** and **multimodal AI**; I am currently deepening my expertise in these evolving fields.

Alongside my academic training in paediatrics, I have participated in **international coursework** and **clinical training**, including programs associated with Harvard University, Cambridge University Hospitals, and the University of North Carolina. I am also active in **student leadership, peer review**, and **interdisciplinary project**.

I have undertaken both **wet and dry laboratory** work. During my practical laboratory training, I systematically learnt relevant techniques such as Western blotting and plasmid extraction, as well as experimental methodology. In addition, I have engaged in data analysis, working on datasets including **NHANES**, **GBD**, as well as **bibliometric analysis** and **metagenomic sequencing**, and have published related articles. More details are available on my [CV](/cv/) and [publications](/publications/).
